About

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT EVENT 2022

We gather organizations, companies, governments, academia, and the people to come and work together for a better planet and society for all. Our organization was established in 2017 in Indonesia, after an MoU between Ministry of Maritime Affairs and Fisheries and DAAD Germany. Sustainable Development Event has now become a global movement and we are pround to present our 3rd and biggest event yet in the City of Rotterdam, Netherlands.
